Available commands:
<amount/100> <player> [<player>] [-] [*]:
          update <player>'s score by <amount/100>
          `-` denotes an incorrect answer; subtracts provided amount.
          `*` denotes daily double; doubles provided amount.
          e.g. `10 a-` subracts $1,000 from player a.
undo:     undo the previous score entry
history:  show the history of score entries
regular:  enter regular Jeopardy round
double:   enter Double Jeopardy round
help:     show this helpful info
